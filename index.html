üì± Getting Started

How do I add my first product?
Tap the "+" button on the main screen, enter product details (name, brand, quantity), set an expiration date, and select a list (Fridge, Pantry, etc.).
How do I create custom lists?
During onboarding, you can select up to 3 lists from our suggestions. You can also create custom lists by going to Settings ‚Üí Manage Lists.
How do I set up expiration reminders?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Notifications and enable notifications. The app will automatically remind you when products are about to expire.
Can I track different types of products?
Yes! House Organizer supports food, drinks, cleaning supplies, clothing, wine bottles, and more. Each type has specific fields for better organization.

üì∏ AI Image Recognition

How does photo recognition work?
Take a photo of a product, and our AI will automatically identify it and suggest details like name, brand, and expiration date. This feature is optional and requires your permission.
Is my photo data private?
Yes! Photos are only sent to our AI service for analysis and are not stored. The analysis happens in real-time and your photos remain private.
What if the AI doesn't recognize my product?
You can still manually enter all the product details. The AI is just a helpful suggestion tool to speed up data entry.

üîß Troubleshooting

The app crashes when I open it
Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the problem persists, restart your device. If issues continue, contact support.
Notifications aren't working
Check that notifications are enabled in Settings ‚Üí Notifications, and that the app has permission in iOS Settings ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí House Organizer.
iCloud sync isn't working
Ensure you're signed into iCloud and have sufficient storage. Check Settings ‚Üí iCloud ‚Üí iCloud Drive is enabled.
Voice commands don't work
Make sure Siri is enabled in iOS Settings ‚Üí Siri & Search. Try saying "Hey Siri" first to test if Siri is working.
App is slow or laggy
Try closing other apps to free up memory. If you have many products, consider organizing them into more specific lists.
Data disappeared
Check if iCloud sync is enabled and you're signed into the same iCloud account. Your data should sync automatically across devices.

‚òÅÔ∏è Data & Privacy

Is my data private and secure?
Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect personal information or share your data with third parties. iCloud sync is optional and user-controlled.
How do I enable iCloud sync?
Go to Settings ‚Üí iCloud Sync and toggle it on. This will sync your data across all your Apple devices using your personal iCloud account.
Can I export my data?
Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Data Management ‚Üí Export Data to create a backup of all your product information.
How do I delete all my data?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Data Management ‚Üí Clear All Data. This will permanently delete all your products and lists. This action cannot be undone.

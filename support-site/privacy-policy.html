House Organizer

Last Updated: January 10, 2025

Introduction

House Organizer ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

  • Product Data: Names, brands, categories, quantities, and expiration dates of products you track
  • Images: Photos you take of products for identification purposes (optional)
  • Lists: Custom product lists you create (e.g., "Fridge", "Pantry", "Bathroom")
  • Preferences: App appearance settings, notification preferences, and language settings

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app (features used, time spent)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Onboarding Status: Whether you have completed the initial setup process

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identifiers such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Browsing history or other app usage outside of our app
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Core Functionality: Track your products, manage lists, and send expiration reminders
  • App Improvement: Understand how users interact with features to improve the app
  • Personalization: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Image Analysis: Process product images using AI services to help identify products (when you choose to use this feature)

Third-Party Services

OpenAI Vision API

  • Purpose: Analyze product images to automatically identify products
  • Data Shared: Only images you explicitly choose to analyze
  • Data Retention: Images are processed and not stored by OpenAI
  • Privacy: Subject to OpenAI's privacy policy

Apple Services

  • iCloud: Optional data synchronization across your devices (when enabled)
  • Notifications: Local notifications for product expiration reminders
  • Siri Integration: Voice commands for adding products and checking lists

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All your data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData
  • Data is encrypted using iOS's built-in security features
  • You have full control over your data

Cloud Storage (Optional)

  • If you enable iCloud sync, data is stored in your personal iCloud account
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time

Security Measures

  • Data is protected by iOS app sandboxing
  • No data is transmitted to our servers
  • API keys are securely managed and not exposed to users

Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

  • Delete Data: You can delete individual products, lists, or all data through the app settings
  • Export Data: You can export your data through the app's data management features
  • Opt-Out: You can disable notifications, image analysis, and iCloud sync at any time

Access and Correction

  • You can view and modify all your data directly in the app
  • No account required - all data is stored locally on your device

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
